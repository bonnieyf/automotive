Asset Management & Maintenance

When you own several expensive, complicated assets it's essential to maximize their benefits through a combination of the right hardware and interconnected software to secure the best ROI.

Challenge

The manufacturing environment presents a number of challenges to managers and operators.

  1. Limited connectivity restricts remote access support.
  2. Unstable network connectivity can interrupt asset management and make it difficult to provide the remote support required.
  3. Poor battery performance of devices can cause downtime and loss of productivity.
  4. Harsh environments mean you need specialist, rugged devices.

Solution

Getac's rugged devices and seamless, easily integrated software connect to how your manufacturing facility works now - finding ways to improve asset management workflows and increase productivity.

  1. Perform predictive maintenance to reduce asset downtime.
  2. Gather real-time data to identify events that may cause issues in the future.
  3. Use built-rugged devices that stand up to the factory environment.
  4. Dual battery capability can last a whole shift and beyond - reducing device downtime.
