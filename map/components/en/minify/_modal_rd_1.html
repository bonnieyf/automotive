Validation & Testing

Today's vehicles are becoming more sophisticated, with onboard computers, electrical power and complex architecture. Every aspect of a vehicle must be thoroughly tested and validated before production - meaning engineers need devices that can work in tandem with complex systems.

Challenge

Testing doesn't just put a demand on the vehicles - it can also put strain on the devices used to perform checks, scanning and calibration. From extreme weather testing to the bumps, knocks and drops in the test lab, devices must be able to support engineers with smooth, precise software that can interface with complicated instruments and stand up to the rugged environment.

Solution

Getac's rugged devices are perfectly suited to the test lab and have been used to support multiple projects in R&D.

  1. Extreme Weather Ready – Getac devices are certified to perform in rain, dust, shock, vibration and withstand both extreme high and low temperatures.
  2. Specialized Ports – Our devices are equipped with ports that support a wide range of calibration, measurement and industry specific tools.
  3. Secure – Protect your sensitive data with biometric security and complete control of your devices.
  4. Versatile – Rugged hardware works in conjunction with manufacturer engineering software to perform scanning, cataloguing and ECU programming uninterrupted.

Benefits

The test lab is a critical part of a vehicle's journey from concept to production. It's a place you need rugged devices you can put your full trust in, that's why we've built our range of devices to perform in R&D.

  1. Cable-Free Connectivity – Connect directly to the vehicle, VCI and R&D infrastructure via fast dual WLAN and Bluetooth.
  2. Global Certification and Support – Our devices are in use by major OEMs, are certified for use globally and backed by our world-class support and services.
  3. Optimized Screen Sizes – No matter your needs, our wide range of devices have large screen sizes to support tasks such as Test Data, Thermal Battery Testing and 3D prototyping.
  4. Flexible Connectivity – Our configurable port options work with the industry's leading testing and measurement devices.
