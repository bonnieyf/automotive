Inventory & Warehouse Management

The modern manufacturing warehouse is critical in ensuring operational efficiency in the manufacturing process - from managing movement of component parts from incoming goods to outgoing end product.

Challenge

Many warehouses use a Warehouse Management System (WMS) to control their inventory levels, and many are moving towards automation to achieve efficiency and cost-reduction - but you need devices that can match their sophistication.

Modern warehouse devices must be able to:

  1. Capture, record and manage inventory flow.
  2. Perform order processing, picking/packing tasks and capture loading and unloading.
  3. Automate material handling where humans and robots are required to work
  4. Integrate with Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems, Automated Guided Vehicles.
  5. Work with barcodes or RFID readers.
  6. Go mobile with workers for truly integrated operations.
  7. Stand up to tough factory floor tasks and environments.

Solution

Getac's solution for inventory and warehouse management integrates seamlessly with the leading inventory management, ERP and WMS software. Our rugged devices have both the computing power and tough construction required for you to work at ease, providing the ideal platform for inventory and warehouse management.
