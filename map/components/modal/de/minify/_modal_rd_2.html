Extreme Conditions & Environmental Testing

Modern vehicles have to stand up to extreme conditions - whether that's the rigours of a frozen road or a hot, humid sub-continental environment. To thoroughly test these conditions, you need a device that can stand up to the same challenges.

Challenge

Not all devices are able to survive in harsh conditions - whether they're simply not built-rugged from the start or have to be put in a case to prevent damage. High-humidity, low temperatures, searing heat - each has its challenges, not to mention risk of drops, knocks or damage in the laboratory.

Solution

Getac is the device of choice for several OEM's to take out into the field, connect with measurement devices and perform as a data acquisition device. They are designed to work in harsh and extreme ambient temperatures, endure high levels of vibration - ensuring there is no costly disruption, device replacement or a need to repeat the test.

Benefits

Our compact, rugged-devices have a number of features that make them perfectly suited to any environment:

  1. Built-rugged construction permeates every level of our devices.
  2. Fast, powerful processors enable rapid working and calculation for the most precise data.
  3. High ease-of-use and device familiarity ensures seamlessly operability by all members of your team.
  4. A range of configurations and accessories means our devices can be modulated to suit any task or environment.
