Intelligent Planning

Artificial Intelligence is everywhere - now you can use it to automate your workshop booking and loading processes. You can greatly reduce the time it takes for customers to book their vehicles in, and save as much as 25% working time to perform the same workshop loading tasks.

Challenge

In many cases workshop planning is still performed with manual and paper-based processes. This slows down operations, wastes time, increases cost and firmly places the responsibility on the service team's shoulders to make critical business decisions, but in reality they are setup to fail. Even the best laid plans can change, and if you can't adapt fast you may deliver poor customer experience and see retention rates drop.

Solution

Getac Automotive delivers both rugged hardware and seamless software to create a smarter running workshop and optimized aftersales.

  1. Real-time processing and distribution of technician duties enables you to avoid resource bottlenecks often caused by low-visibility manual planning.
  2. Our intelligent workshop planning turbocharges calculations of complex workflows to meet customer need - with AI constantly self-learning the unique demands of your workshop.
  3. Utilizes Open API architecture and is RPA compliant.
  4. Uses AI to plan workorders and assign tasks automatically, planning in a way that ensures handover time to the customer will be met and takes into account technician certification and capability.
  5. Our software can adapt instantly in case of sickness to reassign jobs, can take online bookings and planning in teams.
  6. Can work as part of multi or single-point dealerships, constantly generating productivity and efficiency data for management teams.

Benefits

Smarter workshops deliver optimized aftersales, it's why we've combined rugged hardware with seamless planning software in a single platform.

  1. Easy and intuitive planning dashboard with visual alerts and full automation for total accuracy.
  2. Customers and service reception staff can book work and allocate technicians in a few clicks for total convenience and speed.
  3. An adaptable Logic Editor ensures your unique workflows and OEM policies are at the forefront of the planning process and not missed.
  4. Ensure courtesy vehicles, special tools or work-bays are available when you need them
  5. Dashboard and statistical reporting tracks performance and gives you visibility of your whole operation when you need it.
  6. Seamlessly integrated to OEM, dealership and workshop platforms to speed the processes up and receive information in realtime without the inconvenience of logging in and out of multiple systems.
