EVHC

Every Industry is digitalizing, from hailing a taxi to accessing healthcare provider information. Automotive is no different, with customer expectation and behavior driving the need for change. Electronic Vehicle Health Checks (EVHC) enable you to provide a paperless and transparent experience for both customers and service reception team - ensuring the handover and walk around inspection process is kept to a minimum for a better customer experience and a heightened opportunity for upselling wear & tear parts.

Challenge

Paper-based walk arounds take a long time to perform. Once the customer has signed, the service advisor will have to key the notes back into the DMS. This creates double the work and allows the Service Advisor to skip OEM or Dealer standard processes, potentially leaving the dealership exposed. On top of the extra work and risk of non-compliance, customers also have a worsened experience as they must spend time waiting as the Advisor refers back to the DMS for information.

Solution

Getac Automotive gives workshops the ability to perform easy, integrated and paperless EVHCs.

  1. 100% Vehicle Inspections – The Getac EVHC platform mandates that all checks are carried out in accordance with OEM standards, delivering customer peace of mind and more revenue opportunities for the dealer.
  2. Quick Intuitive User Interface – Getac's EVHC platform streamlines the whole process, allowing time estimates and accurate handover times to be generated for a quick decisions.
  3. Fits how you work – Our software is operating system agnostic, with a highly configurable and flexible Open API.
  4. See the full picture – Get a dashboard and management console to interrogate throughput, compliance and efficiency at a site, group, national or global level.

Benefits

Getac Automotive's combination of seamless software and rugged hardware come together to deliver a number of workshop benefits:

  1. Move from slow paper processes to a fast, efficient tablet ecosystem.
  2. Control of health check process.
  3. Give your customers more visibility.
  4. Increase parts per repair order and labor hours sold.
  5. Business intelligence shows where improvements can be made.
