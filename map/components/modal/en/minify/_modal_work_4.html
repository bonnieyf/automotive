PC Based Diagnostics

Did you know there are between 60 and 100 Electronic Control Units on a modern vehicle? Each one requires dedicated diagnostics to conduct a thorough check - and they keep updating. Some happen over the air but others must be done in-person by a diagnostic technician.

Challenge

A comprehensive diagnostic check of a vehicle can take between 1-3 hours and if your workshop deals with products from multiple OEM's, you need a solution that can keep up with both the latest updates and the tough, fast-paced workshop environment.

Solution

Getac Automotive helps you get back time wasted and maximize your technician time with rugged hardware and seamless software.

  1. Our tablet PC's connect to the vehicle fast via integrated WLAN or Giga-bit Ethernet.
  2. Getac's PC based diagnostics consider all aspects of the diagnostic process and ensures optimal viewing.
  3. Using rugged, mobile technology allows more time to be spent working on the vehicle without being tied to a workbench.

Benefits

Getac devices are the optimal tool for both dealership workshops and mobile technicians.

  1. Cable Free Connectivity – Our devices connect directly to the vehicle, VCI and workshop computer systems with dual WLAN and Bluetooth.
  2. Rugged as a Service – Subscription models are available - enabling you to spread the investment and take advantage of a strong ROI.
  3. Global Certification and Support – Our devices are certified for global use, supporting major OEM programs and backed by world class service and support.
  4. Optimal Screen Size – Larger screen sizes allow users to easily view datalogging, technical publications, wiring diagrams and job card information.
