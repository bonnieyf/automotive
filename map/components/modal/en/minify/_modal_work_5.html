With the average vehicle car parc age getting older - in the US it is circa 11.8 years, Germany 9.8 years and Russia 13 years - upkeep of existing vehicles will require higher maintenance investment. This means access to comprehensive repair and maintenance information from the OEM.

Challenge

Right to repair is a hot button topic across a number of industries - including automotive. OEM's are responsible for providing technical parts and service information to its own network, customers and independent repair facilities - either through technical portals and websites for its franchised service points or online subscription services for the independent aftermarket.

Solution

Getac Automotive delivers vehicle code details, non-emission related information, re-programming or flashing for all makes and models, directly to the technician's rugged device - improving vehicle servicing and repair efficiency - to create a seamless technician experience.

Benefits

Getac Automotive is the proven workshop and diagnostic tool of choice for OEM's around the world.

With Getac's optimized solution you get devices that can:

  1. Become the single platform for repair order tasks, time-clocking, technical data and diagnostics.
  2. Connect with WWAN for OEM cloud-based technical platforms when out in the field or experiencing poor WLAN capabilities.
  3. Confidential technical information secured with Getac optional features such as RFID, Device Monitoring and multi- factor authentication.
  4. Stand up to any workshop task with built-rugged construction and seamless software.
