Intelligent Planning

By digitalizing your job cards and connecting to other workshop systems, such as the DMS, parts and technical, you can gain an extra 10% efficiency and save 12 hours per month for the technician versus paper and manual based workflows.

Challenge

Technicians often waste time locating vehicles from the service parking area and this could take as much as 10 minutes per job card. For an average size of workshop (10 Service bays) over the month, this could add up to 146 hours of non-productive time. As well as this, technicians clocking on and off job cards can cause up to 12 hours per month to be wasted accessing centrally located clocking systems.

Solution

Getac Automotive delivers both rugged hardware and seamless software to create a smarter running workshop and optimized aftersales.

  1. Paperless – Digitize paper-based workflows with digital repair orders that have task allocation, time-clocking, internal messenger and direct link to parts and technical information (just in time and real time).
  2. With the use of proximity technology and the technicians Getac tablet, the technician will be able to quickly locate the vehicle in a busy forecourt.

Benefits

Smarter workshops deliver optimized aftersales, it's why we've combined rugged hardware with seamless planning software in a single platform.

  1. Your Getac tablet is a single, mobile platform for repair order tasks, time-clocking, technical data and diagnostics.
  2. Enhance compliance with better visibility of the real-time, paperless process.
  3. Connects to your workshop through an Open API that is operating system agnostic.
  4. Our ‘Just in time’ methodology delivers greater convenience and efficiency.
  5. Mobilize your staff to be productive and take on a greater number of revenue generating jobs with far less idle time.
